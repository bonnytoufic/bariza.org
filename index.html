Who We Are

Bariza Pulmonary Support Services LLC is a Southern California respiratory consulting company founded by Bonny Toufic, BBA, BSRC, RRT, RCP. Our mission is to improve respiratory care through clinical consulting, staff education, pulmonary program development, and respiratory equipment support.

Healthcare Services

Respiratory Consulting

Clinical consulting for hospitals, rehabilitation centers, skilled nursing facilities and home health agencies.

Staff Education

Evidence-based respiratory education for nurses, caregivers and healthcare professionals.

Patient Education

COPD education, oxygen therapy, CPAP/BiPAP coaching and caregiver training.

Pulmonary Programs

Support developing respiratory care programs that improve patient outcomes.

Respiratory Equipment Solutions

We provide education, implementation support, clinical guidance and consulting related to respiratory equipment used across acute care, post-acute care, home health and rehabilitation settings. Future partnerships with manufacturers and durable medical equipment providers will further expand our equipment support capabilities.
